Jakarta's Best Culinary

Worth to try Jakarta Culinary - If you are travelling to Jakarta, and you are about to eat, maybe its not completed without trying some local culinary, right? So here is the list for you to try some!!!

Kerak Telor

Kerak Telor is one of the most famous food of jakarta, if you are strolling around Central Jakarta, you will found it almost everywhere, especially in Historical Places, like Kota Tua, Monas, and others. The main composition of Kerak Telor is sticky rice, egg, some seasoning, and chili (optional). And for the garnish it has fried onions, and also Serundeng. And it is wrapped by rice paper. Sounds delicious right? yet it is only cost Rp 15.000 - Rp 20.000 / pcs. Like to try?


Soto Betawi

Soto Betawi is the best Jakarta's food with sauce or kuah, it's so good to try especially when the rain comes, so its the best to serve it hot. The ingredients of Soto Betawi are coconut milk, full cream milk, few spices, and meet. But, sometimes its different in certain shop, like some of them are using beef, beef tongue, beef innards, or other parts of beef, or even chicken. Maybe it's not about the topping. it is about the sauce, another Soto than Soto betawi such as Soto Mie, didn't use full cream milk as the sauce. They only use chicken broth and spices for the sauce. For this food, you can find it almost everywhere, even outer Jakarta, like Bekasi or Depok. Soto Betawi cost Rp 15.000 - Rp 30.000 per bowl, Indonesian usually serve it with rice as their main food.


Ketoprak

Ketoprak is the kind of food you really want to taste when you're in Jakarta. The portion per plate is going to make your belly full. The composition of Ketoprak are lontong (it is kind of rice cake) , ground peanut, brown sugar, and a little bit of water to make it into peanut sauce. And also white noodle, long beans, cucumber, crackers, and sweet soy sauce for the complement or topping. You can find Ketoprak in everywhere around Jakarta, mostly at the side of Jakarta's Street. It cost Rp 10.000 until Rp 15.000 per portion.
